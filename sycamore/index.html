Sycamore

A place of meeting and working and building a life in common.

We invite you to help shape the future of Sycamore, a big camelback double that has for seven years been an outpost of New Orleans counterpower.

Sycamore has done, been and survived many things; now we are looking to make the largest shift yet, larger than anything we've done before. We are expanding our vision, deepening our commitments and multiplying our capacities.

Who We Are

Sycamore is a "hub", a place to ground ourselves, to meet and build friendly energy against the domination of solitude. Every hub tends to its own growth as well as the growth of others, each constructed in line with its makers' means, needs and desire for a meaningful version of happiness.

What We're Doing

By completing the renovation of the front of the house, we want to expand Sycamore to include a gathering hall, partisan library and more, expanding our resources as a space for encounter, experimentation and organization.

How We're Going To Do It

Over the course of eight weeks in September and October we can accomplish these ambitious goals... together. So it is we are writing to ask for your time, materials, skills, food, companionship and even money.

Please let us know how you would like to contribute.
